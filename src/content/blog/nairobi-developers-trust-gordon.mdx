Fitting out a single home is one thing. Delivering kitchens, wardrobes and bathrooms across an entire development — on time, on budget, and to a consistent standard — is another entirely. Over the years, property developers across Nairobi have come to rely on me for exactly that. Here's why.

## Consistency at scale

When you're selling or letting multiple units, every kitchen has to match the show home. Developers trust me because I deliver the same quality and finish across ten, twenty or fifty units — no drop-off, no surprises. The last unit looks exactly as good as the first.

## Reliable timelines

In development, time is money. A delayed fitout holds up handover, sales and rental income. I plan production and installation around the construction programme, keep developers updated at every milestone, and deliver when I say I will. That reliability is worth as much as the product itself.

## Units that help sell homes

A well-fitted kitchen and quality built-in wardrobes are among the strongest selling points in Nairobi's property market. Buyers and tenants notice quality immediately. I design and finish units that make properties show beautifully and move faster — which is exactly what developers care about.

## One point of contact

Developers juggle dozens of suppliers and contractors. Working with me means one direct, accountable point of contact for all the cabinetry and fitout work — no chasing a faceless company, no being passed around. I take ownership of the brief and see it through.

## Designs that balance cost and appeal

I understand the developer's balancing act: the finish has to impress buyers while staying within a viable budget per unit. I help specify materials and designs that look premium and photograph well, without over-spending where it won't be noticed. That commercial awareness is something developers value.

## Built for the climate

Across a whole development, durability matters even more — call-backs and warranty issues are expensive and damage reputations. I use moisture-resistant materials and quality hardware throughout, so the units hold up and the developer's name stays strong.

## After-sales they can rely on

Handover isn't the end. I provide dependable after-sales support, so if anything needs adjusting after residents move in, it's handled professionally and quickly. That protects the developer's relationship with their buyers.
