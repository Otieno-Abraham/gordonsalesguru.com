One of the biggest worries people have before starting a kitchen, wardrobe or bathroom project is the unknown: how does it actually work, and how long will it take? I keep my process simple, transparent and personal — you deal with me at every stage. Here's exactly what to expect.

## Step 1: Enquiry

It starts with a message. You reach out on WhatsApp or through the form on this site and tell me a little about your project — the room, the style you like, roughly when you'd want it done. There's no obligation and no pressure. I reply within two hours during business hours.

## Step 2: Design consultation

Next, we meet — either at your space or at my showroom on Mombasa Road. This is where I take careful measurements and, just as importantly, listen. How do you use the room? What frustrates you about it now? What look do you love? From this I shape a design that's genuinely yours.

## Step 3: Custom quote

I then prepare a clear, itemised quote based on your design — materials, finishes, hardware and a realistic timeline, all spelled out. No vague figures, no hidden extras. You'll know exactly what you're getting and when. If you want to adjust the scope to suit your budget, we do that together before anything is ordered.

## Step 4: Production & shipping

Once you approve the design and quote, your units go into production, built precisely to the agreed specification. I keep you updated through the key milestones so you're never left wondering. When everything is ready, it's carefully shipped to your location — whether that's around Nairobi or across East Africa.

## Step 5: Installation & handover

Finally, my team installs everything to a flawless finish. We make sure doors align, drawers glide, and every detail is right before we hand over. I walk you through your finished space, and I stand behind the work with dependable after-sales support if you ever need it.

## Why the personal approach matters

Because I manage every stage myself, nothing falls through the cracks. There's no call centre, no being passed between departments — just one expert who knows your project inside out and cares about getting it right. That's how I've built trust with homeowners and developers across the region.

## Ready when you are

Whether you know exactly what you want or you're just starting to think about it, the first step is the same: a simple conversation.
