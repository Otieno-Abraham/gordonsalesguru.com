In a Kenyan apartment, every square metre counts — and nothing wastes space quite like a free-standing wardrobe. Built-in wardrobes, designed around your exact room, are the smartest way to get serious storage without sacrificing floor space. Here are the designs that work hardest in compact bedrooms.

## Go floor-to-ceiling

The first rule of maximising space is using all of it — right up to the ceiling. Free-standing wardrobes leave a dusty, useless gap on top. A built-in runs to the ceiling, with the high section used for off-season clothes, luggage and rarely needed items. That alone can add a third more storage in the same footprint.

## Tackle the corners

Awkward corners are where apartments lose the most space. A built-in can turn a dead corner into an L-shaped run of storage or a corner hanging unit, reclaiming room that would otherwise be wasted. Made-to-measure means nothing is left over.

## Slim, space-saving doors

In a tight bedroom, sliding doors are often the answer — they don't swing into the room, so you can place the bed right alongside. A mirrored sliding panel does double duty: a full-length mirror plus the visual trick of making the room feel larger and brighter.

## Design the interior to your life

The real space-saving happens inside. I design built-ins with:

- **Double-hang rails** to fit twice as many shirts and trousers in the same height.
- **Adjustable shelves** that flex as your needs change.
- **Internal drawers** for accessories, keeping surfaces clear.
- **Angled shoe racks** so footwear stays visible and organised.

Every centimetre is allocated to something you actually own.

## Integrate with the room

A built-in can wrap over the bed or around a doorway, turning otherwise unusable wall space into storage. In studio and one-bed apartments, this kind of integrated design is transformative — it makes a small space feel considered and calm rather than cramped.

## Keep it light

Light finishes — soft whites, pale wood tones — keep a small bedroom feeling open. Add discreet LED lighting inside and the wardrobe feels premium as well as practical.

## Built for the long haul

Apartments in Nairobi see real humidity, so I use moisture-resistant boards and quality hardware that won't sag or stick. A built-in is a long-term investment in your home, and it should last like one.

## Designed for your exact apartment

No two apartments are identical, which is exactly why a tailored built-in beats anything off the shelf. I measure, plan around your room's quirks, and build storage that fits like it was always meant to be there.
